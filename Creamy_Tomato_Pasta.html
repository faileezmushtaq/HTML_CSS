Creamy Tomato Pasta

Ingredients

  • Diced onion - This really is optional
  • Garlic - Fresh garlic cloves,finely diced.
  • Tomato paste - Needs to be cooked out for a few mins.
  • Cream - A single or light cream
  • Parmesan cheese - Finely grated
  • Black Pepper - According to taste
  • Lemon juice - Make sure the heat is low when adding.

Steps

  1. In a large pan, fry the onion in olive oil and butter over a medium heat until softened, 4 - 5 minutes. Don't let them colour so turn the heat down if you need to.
  2. Add the garlic and pepper and mix well, cook for a further minute.
  3. Add the tomato paste and let it cook for a minute or two, stirring.
  4. Add the cream, you don't want a lot of heat here so switch to a low heat. The cream is at a very gentle simmer. Whisk together. Add the lemon juice and whisk in quickly.
  5. It will look a bit dodgy at first but it will come together to a nice orange sauce like this. I love a flat whisk for this.
  6. Add the grated parmesan cheese in a few separate lots, letting it melt into the sauce between each addition.
  7. Add your cooked pasta of choice straight into the sauce with a splash of pasta water and let it gently bubble away for a minute or two to thicken.
