Lemonade

Ingredients

  • Lemons: 6–9 medium lemons (yields 1.5 cups of fresh juice)
  • Sugar: 1 to 1.25 cups granulated sugar
  • Water: 1 cup (for the syrup) + 6 to 7 cups of ice-cold water (for diluting)
  • Ice & Garnishes: Fresh mint leaves or lemon slices

Steps

  1. Make the Simple Syrup:In a small saucepan, combine 1 cup of sugar and 1 cup of water. Heat over medium-low heat, stirring constantly until the sugar is completely dissolved into a clear liquid. Remove from heat and let it cool for 10 minutes
  2. Juice the Lemons:Roll the lemons firmly on the counter to soften them (this helps yield more juice). Cut in half and squeeze until you have about 1.5 cups of juice. Remove any seeds, but you can leave the pulp.
  3. Mix and Serve:Pour the fresh lemon juice and the cooled simple syrup into a pitcher. Add 6 to 7 cups of cold water. Stir well, taste, and add more water if it is too tart or sweet for your preference. Serve over ice and garnish with lemon wheels or fresh mint.
